About Me


I am an aspiring data scientist with a passion for leveraging data-driven insights to solve complex problems. With my expertise in areas such as machine learning, statistical analysis, and data visualization, I strive to extract actionable intelligence from vast amounts of data.
Born on 2nd March 2001, I developed an early interest in the intersection of mathematics, programming, and data analysis. I embarked on a journey to acquire knowledge and skills in the field of data science, ultimately aiming to contribute to advancements in various domains.

Academic Background

I hold a Degree in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science from Centurion University of Technology. During my academic journey, I focused on courses and projects related to data analysis, machine learning, and statistical modeling. I honed my skills in programming languages such as Python, R, and SQL, demonstrating a strong understanding of data manipulation and analysis techniques.

Professional Experience

I have gained practical experience in the data science field through internships and research projects. I have collaborated with diverse teams, leveraging data-driven approaches to solve real-world problems. Notably, I contributed to the development of the Kobold AI API - a Realtime API to communicate with Kobold AI GPT and worked on Automatic AI-Based Farm Moisture Analyzer.

Personal Interests

Corona Dashboard

A Plotly Dashboard project that analyzes and visually presents COVID-19 data, providing insights into the pandemic. It offers interactive charts and graphs, empowering users to explore and understand the impact of the virus

Mood Melody

Mood Melody is a personal project that combines Mood Detection and AI-generated Song Recommendations. It utilizes advanced algorithms to analyze users' moods and suggest personalized songs, creating a unique music experience tailored to individual emotions.

Power Bi Game Dashboard

The Power BI Dashboard is a personal project that analyzes and showcases video game sales data. It provides insightful visualizations and statistics, allowing for comprehensive analysis of the gaming industry's sales performance.
